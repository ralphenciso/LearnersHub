About Learner's Hub

Learner's hub is your online library in the Digital World. This website collates numerous available learning and educational resources online to serve as a reference to different kinds of students and learners. It is also serves as social platform to bring together people who are active in online learning. Online learning has a lot of advantages as compared to older traditional means. However, it sometimes requires great discipline and motivation to be effective. Creating a social community and bringing together people in local areas together to collaborate and study together can get greatly help in this area and is one of the main goals of this site.

World-class education on a click

With the number of online learning sites available today, picking one and finding the right resources fit to you can be become confusing. Learner's Hub can help by filtering the best and most up to date courses available in each particular subject based on student reviews. The website also provides learning paths utilizing multiple resources as a guide for learners studying a subject covering a broad range of topics. These guides will ensure effective progression in learning and serve as a gateway to world-class education on a click of button.

A community of passionate learners

One of the main goals of this site is to bring together people who loves learning. Technology has greatly enabled the field of education, extending its reach to more people than ever before. This however shouldn't mean the end of traditional learning. There is, without doubt, great value in learning together with other people and actual physical interactions are irreplaceable. Bringing this aspect of traditional learning to online learning can create a very effective,engaging and enjoyable learning process. This goal can be achieved by this website by acting as a social platform. Enabling people to share what subjects they are currently learning and where they are from, in hope of creating groups that could meet up in a certain location.
